About Us

ON BEHALF OF AVOCADO SUSHI, we welcome you to our family of restaurants. At Avocado Sushi, we have created a sophisticated, yet welcoming, atmosphere in which we create exceptional sushi, sashimi, nigrinizushi, cooked Asian dishes and more for your enjoyment. Dedicated to complete customer satisfaction, we hope that you will think of us as your favorite, local restaurant destination, no matter what the occasion!


At Avocado Sushi, we would like to be your guide on your journey into the world of sushi. Whether you are a novice to the sushi experience, or a seasoned pro, glance through our Sushi Guide for definitions, tips, photos and suggestions to get you started.

We hope the information there helps open up the world of sushi for you. Of course, your server always makes an excellent in-person guide as well!
